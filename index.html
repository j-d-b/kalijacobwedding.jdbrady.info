Kali and Jacob Wedding 2023

This website contains information about Kali and Jacob's wedding at Sleepy Hollow Inn in Huntington Vermont, May 20th, 2023.

The wedding will be casual, small, and simple. The focus is on hanging out and spending time together. Lodging at the venue will be provided to all guests on Friday and Saturday night and we encourage you to stay both nights.

Meals will be provided but consider bringing snacks or additional nutrition.

We live with a strong emphasis on sustainability and living in harmony with the planet. The venue is entirely solar-powered, we will serve primarily local and organic food, and encourage offsetting carbon emissions from travel (in lieu of a wedding gift). See Resources for information about carbon offsets for travel. In line with sustainability goals, we do not want gifts. Please help us minimize our impact to the earth.

Activities

We will be outside most of the weekend, so please pack for spring conditions in Vermont (variable temperature, mud possible). We plan to do some or all of the following activities, depending on weather and trail conditions:

  • Wedding ceremony and party.
  • Group run.
  • Nature walk and birding (peak migration in Vermont).
  • Astronomical viewing.
  • Games.
